MAKIN' Bacon

Instructions

Makin' Bacon is a "skill-shot/target practice" style game that is similar in functionality to NES DuckHunt.

Two players take turns "shooting" targets(ideally: flying pigs) Gameplay uses a combination of aiming via the mouse cursor and firing with the spacebar. User goal is to hit the most targets during a 30 second interval or "round".
